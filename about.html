ЁЯУШ About the App (Class 10 тАУ English)

Rankiya is an educational mobile application designed especially for Bihar Board Class 10 students.

The app helps students prepare effectively for board examinations through chapter-wise MCQ practice, instant results, and simple, easy-to-understand solutions.

Our Purpose

The purpose of Rankiya is to make Class 10 exam preparation simple, effective, and well-structured. The app focuses on regular practice and concept clarity to help students perform better in their board exams.

Key Features

  • MCQs based on Bihar Board Class 10 exam pattern
  • Chapter-wise practice sets
  • Instant score and performance analysis
  • Clear and simple solutions
  • Lightweight and student-friendly interface

Who Can Use This App

  • Bihar Board Class 10 students
  • Students preparing for board examinations
  • Learners practicing through MCQs
